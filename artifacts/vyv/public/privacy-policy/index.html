PRIVACY POLICY

VYV (Visualize Your Vibe)

1. Introduction

Welcome to VYV ("we", "our"). VYV helps you improve wellness and personal productivity. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information obtained through the app, including photos, posts, and social activity. By using VYV, you agree to the data processing as described in this policy. The application is available in multiple languages and can be used in the language the user prefers.

2. Information We Collect

• Data you provide (name, email, profile picture, posts, images, comments). • Data generated by your app usage (schedules, habits, statistics, interactions). • Technical data (IP address, device type, operating system). • If you choose to link wearables or health apps, data such as steps, sleep hours, or heart rate may be read.

3. How We Use Your Information

• To enable posting and viewing content. • To personalize your experience and offer recommendations. • To analyze app usage and improve it. • To comply with legal requirements or prevent abuse.

4. Sharing Information

• We do not sell your personal information. • Public content may be viewed based on the privacy settings you choose. • We may share aggregated data (without identifying you) for statistical purposes.

5. Your Rights

• You can decide what you share and with whom. • You can edit or delete your profile or posts. • You can revoke permissions from settings.

6. Data Security & Retention

• We protect your information with reasonable security measures. • Data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the stated purposes.

7. Minors

If you are under 13, you need parental consent.

8. Changes

We may update this Policy and notify you of significant changes.
